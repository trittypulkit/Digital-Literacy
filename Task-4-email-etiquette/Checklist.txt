Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist

Do's - 
✔ Think before you post — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
✔ Maintain a professional online presence
✔ Protect your privacy by adjusting security settings regularly
✔ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
✔ Use social media positively for learning, networking and skill-building

Don'ts - 
✖ Do not share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number,etc)
✖ Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
✖ Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
✖ Avoid oversharing your daily activities or private life
✖ Do not trust or click on suspicious links or unknown source
