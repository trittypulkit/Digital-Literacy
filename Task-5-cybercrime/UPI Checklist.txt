Stay Safe Online – Checklist

Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank or official authority.

Do not scan QR codes to receive money—you only scan to pay, not to receive.

Verify payment requests carefully before approving any UPI transaction.

Avoid clicking on unknown links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.

Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).

Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions or logging into sensitive accounts.

Regularly check your bank statements and UPI transaction history for any suspicious activity.

Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public computers.

Keep your phone and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.

If You Become a Victim:

Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent assistance
